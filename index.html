Text Elements with different contextual meaning in HTML5

  • <b> - no longer represents boldface but is now used to indicate "stylistically offset" text without conveying importance. Use <b> for keywords, product names and actionable items. The b element on W3
  • <i> - used for text in an "alternate voice or mood". Could be spoken text, thoughts or something similar that doesn't convey importance or emphasis. The i elemenent
  • <strong> - for strong importance, where the content is more important than nearby words The strong element
  • <em> - indicates emphatic stress The em element
  • <small> - used for small print or side comments. Useful for copyright lines or adding a source line to an image The small element
